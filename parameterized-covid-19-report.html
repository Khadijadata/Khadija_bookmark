8 Parameterized COVID-19 report

In this chapter I will focus on parameters, the next step and an important part when it comes to a reproducible analysis. The benefit of using parameters is it’ll help simplify re-using or applying the analysis to different datasets or various categories within the same dataset. I will use COVID-19 data (2022) and use COVID19 cases and COVID19-related deaths. The aim is is to create a parameterized report for the COVID-19 cases and deaths and visualize them in plots.

Firstly, the analysis will be executed with three parameters: The country to which the report applies to, the year that the reported data applies to and the period in months that the report applies to.
